How to get your Strava archive

  1. Log in to Strava on the web.
  2. Go to Settings → My Account.
  3. Under Download or Delete Your Account, click Get Started.
  4. Request your archive — Strava emails you a link (can take hours to days).
  5. Unzip the download, then upload the archive here.

Database import is not yet implemented — this is just the shell for now.
